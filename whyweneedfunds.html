Why We Need Funds?

The trust is a non-profitable organization and is working to provide good quality education to the children especially in rural areas. This trust has a clear vision to impart quality education at par with the urban areas. To provide quality education, the trust has to invest a huge amount of money to make quality education affordable.

THE TRUST IS COMMITTED TO GIVE FREE EDUCATION TO 20% OF THE UNDERPRIVILEGED STUDENTS WITH FREE UNIFORMS AND BOOKS. To make this a sustainable solution, the trust will charge nominal fees from the rest of the students. The proposed school will have all the facilities such as a music class, swimming pool, large play ground with coaches for almost every game along with all the extra-curricular activities at par with the standard schools in urban areas. Furthermore, the quality education needs experienced and skilled faculty and for which, the remunerations will be at par with the urban areas. Further, the trust is also willing to hire education consultants.
